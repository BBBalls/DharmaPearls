Radical 9: 人

人

人 n. man, human, person, people, referring to humans in general. This general term can be nuanced in translation based on context. Usage: 佛在拘薩羅國人間遊行 "the Buddha was in Kauśala touring among the people" DA 29.1.

佛

佛 (Skt. buddha) pn. the Buddha, referring to Gautama. Usage: 佛在拘薩羅國人間遊行 "the Buddha toured among the people of Kośala" DA passim.
